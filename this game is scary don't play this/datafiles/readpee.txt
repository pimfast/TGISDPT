controls:

Left Mouse: Select tower/button
WASD/Arrow Keys: Move player character (important in level 2!)

(when holding a tower)
Left Mouse: Place tower
Middle Mouse: Cancel
Right Mouse: Rotate (only for cannon)


other buttons:
M = mute music
Esc = titlescreen


towers:

archer: standard damage with long range
cannon: fires in a straight line with bigger damage but slower firing rate
knight: itty-bitty range with good damage
saw: same idea as knight. (will be replaced by a cooler tower in the future!)
wizard: quicker and more powerful attacks but at a higher cost
